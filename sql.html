Customer Complaint Analytics

Analyzed 800K+ CFPB-style customer complaints to identify SLA breaches, emerging product risks, and geographic hospots. Used SQL YoY trend analysis, percentile-based outlier detection, and exception reporting with LAG, PERCENTILE_CONT, and conditional aggregation. Delivered 9 recommendations for Ops and compliance, including a seasonal staffing model and vendor risk alerts projected to reduce regulatory exposure.
